What is VolunTeam?


VolunTeam is a web-based platform that allows volunteers and volunteer organizations to connect with each other easily.

Contact Organizations Directly


VolunTeam allows for volunteers to communicate directly with the organizations they are interested in volunteering for or just want to learn more about.

Schedule Volunteering Sessions


VolunTeam also allows organizations to post the days and times they are available for volunteers to choose from and sign up for.

Volunteer With Your Team


Being able to volunteer with people you know is important. That's why VolunTeam makes it possible for you to create a team and invite your friends to volunteer with you!

Have Everything in One Place


VolunTeam keeps everything organized for you. From your volunteer account, you can see everything from organizations you have contacted to the teams you are in to past and future volunteer sessions.
